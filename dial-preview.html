The family dial

A radial-gauge take on the four family dials, instead of the flat bars in the current prototype. Shown in a sample mid-game reading.

At risk Watch On track Target line

Each dial runs from −6 to +6 with the target line at +2. The needle shows where the family currently sits as cards and chance events move the score. This is a visual concept for the physical and on-screen component — colours and range are easy to tune.
