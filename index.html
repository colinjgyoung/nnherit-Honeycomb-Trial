The Honeycomb

A gamified succession session. Fill the honeycomb with scenario cards from the full Succession Dynamics deck, watch the business and family meters respond, weather the chance cards — then turn the board into a transition roadmap.

The board

Business & family meters

Target line — the line the family agrees to clear: 0

Scenario cards — click one, then click a hex · i for the card back

Full 60-card Succession Dynamics deck, 12 cards per challenge. Card colour identifies the challenge; difficulty is a 1–4 rating on each card back. All eight scored dimensions are tracked: Profitability, Continuity, External and Governance feed the business meter; Harmony, Family, Communication and Readiness feed the family meter.
